Truffle Lobster Mac 'n' Cheese

Creamy mac 'n' cheese with succulent lobster and a hint of truffle oil, topped with golden breadcrumbs.

Indulge in a creamy, dreamy mac ‘n’ cheese that’s taken to new heights with sweet, tender lobster chunks folded into a velvety cheese sauce. The subtle earthiness of truffle oil infuses every bite, while the crispy, golden breadcrumbs on top provide the perfect crunch to contrast the rich, melt-in-your-mouth texture of the dish. Each forkful is a luxurious, comforting delight.

Grilled Peach and Burrata Salad

Charred peaches paired with creamy burrata, arugula, and a honey-balsamic drizzle.

A refreshing burst of summer flavors, this salad brings together the caramelized sweetness of charred peaches with the creamy decadence of burrata cheese. The smooth, rich cheese contrasts beautifully with the peppery freshness of arugula, while a honey-balsamic drizzle adds a sweet and tangy finish. Toasted almonds offer a satisfying crunch, making each bite a symphony of textures and flavors.

Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Giant portobello caps stuffed with spinach, ricotta, and parmesan, then roasted to perfection.

These hearty portobello mushroom caps are generously filled with a creamy, savory mixture of spinach, ricotta, and parmesan, then roasted until the filling is bubbling and golden. The earthy, meaty mushrooms provide the perfect base for the rich, indulgent stuffing, creating a dish that is both comforting and full of flavor in every bite.

Crispy Coconut Shrimp Tacos

Coconut-breaded shrimp in soft tortillas, topped with mango salsa and a tangy cilantro-lime crema.

Golden, crunchy coconut-crusted shrimp are nestled in soft, warm tortillas, their sweetness perfectly complemented by a vibrant mango salsa that bursts with freshness. A drizzle of tangy cilantro-lime crema ties everything together, balancing the richness of the shrimp with a refreshing, zesty finish. These tacos are a tropical escape, combining sweet, savory, and crunchy in one perfect bite.

Saffron Risotto with Seared Scallops

Creamy saffron-infused risotto topped with perfectly seared scallops and a lemon zest finish.

Luxuriously creamy risotto infused with the delicate, floral essence of saffron, each bite is a velvety symphony of flavors. The risotto is paired with perfectly seared scallops, their golden crust giving way to a tender, melt-in-your-mouth center. A sprinkle of fresh lemon zest adds a burst of brightness, making this dish a sophisticated balance of richness and freshness.
